### [1] [Integrating Deep RL and Bayesian Inference for ObjectNav in Mobile Robotics](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25366)
*João Castelo-Branco,José Santos-Victor,Alexandre Bernardino*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: 提出了一种结合贝叶斯推理与深度强化学习的混合物体搜索框架，在室内环境中通过维护目标位置的空间信念地图，并训练强化学习策略从概率表示中选择导航动作，提高了搜索成功率和效率。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 移动机器人在室内环境中进行自主物体搜索面临部分可观测性、感知不确定性和探索-导航效率权衡的挑战。传统概率方法依赖手工设计的动作选择启发式规则，而深度强化学习方法虽然能适应但收敛慢且可解释性有限。

Method: 提出混合物体搜索框架，通过贝叶斯推理从校准的物体检测中在线更新目标位置的空间信念地图，并训练强化学习策略直接从该概率表示中选择导航动作。

Result: 在Habitat 3.0的室内仿真环境中评估，与基线策略相比，在两个室内环境中提高了搜索成功率，同时减少了搜索工作量。

Conclusion: 结果表明，将贝叶斯信念估计与学习型动作选择相结合，能够在部分可观测条件下实现更高效可靠的物体搜索行为。

Abstract: Autonomous object search is challenging for mobile robots operating in indoor environments due to partial observability, perceptual uncertainty, and the need to trade off exploration and navigation efficiency. Classical probabilistic approaches explicitly represent uncertainty but typically rely on handcrafted action-selection heuristics, while deep reinforcement learning enables adaptive policies but often suffers from slow convergence and limited interpretability. This paper proposes a hybrid object-search framework that integrates Bayesian inference with deep reinforcement learning. The method maintains a spatial belief map over target locations, updated online through Bayesian inference from calibrated object detections, and trains a reinforcement learning policy to select navigation actions directly from this probabilistic representation. The approach is evaluated in realistic indoor simulation using Habitat 3.0 and compared against developed baseline strategies. Across two indoor environments, the proposed method improves success rate while reducing search effort. Overall, the results support the value of combining Bayesian belief estimation with learned action selection to achieve more efficient and reliable objectsearch behavior under partial observability.

### [2] [A Mentalistic Interface for Probing Folk-Psychological Attribution to Non-Humanoid Robots](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25646)
*Giulio Pisaneschi,Pierpaolo Serio,Estelle Gerbier,Andrea Dan Ryals,Lorenzo Pollini,Mario G. C. A. Cimino*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: 开发了一个实验平台，用于研究人们对非人形机器人意向状态归因，通过模拟机器人、真实任务环境和基于大语言模型的解释层，在不同解释框架下保持行为一致，研究语言和框架如何影响机器人领域意向立场的采用。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究人们如何将意向状态归因于非人形机器人，探索语言和解释框架如何影响人们对机器人行为的理解，特别是在不同解释框架（心理主义、目的论、机械论）下，人们如何采纳意向立场。

Method: 开发了一个实验平台，包含模拟机器人、真实任务环境和基于大语言模型的解释层。该平台能够在保持机器人行为不变的情况下，用心理主义、目的论或机械论三种不同框架来解释相同行为，从而控制变量研究解释框架的影响。

Result: 该平台提供了一个受控的研究环境，能够系统地研究语言和解释框架如何塑造人们对机器人行为的理解，以及如何影响意向立场的采纳。通过保持行为一致而改变解释框架，可以分离出语言和框架对意向归因的独立影响。

Conclusion: 该实验平台为研究机器人领域意向立场的采纳提供了一个有价值的工具，能够帮助理解语言和解释框架在塑造人们对非人形机器人意向状态归因中的关键作用，为未来人机交互和机器人设计提供理论基础。

Abstract: This paper presents an experimental platform for studying intentional-state attribution toward a non-humanoid robot. The system combines a simulated robot, realistic task environments, and large language model-based explanatory layers that can express the same behavior in mentalistic, teleological, or mechanistic terms. By holding behavior constant while varying the explanatory frame, the platform provides a controlled way to investigate how language and framing shape the adoption of the intentional stance in robotics.

### [3] [Fast-dVLA: Accelerating Discrete Diffusion VLA to Real-Time Performance](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25661)
*Wenxuan Song,Jiayi Chen,Shuai Chen,Jingbo Wang,Pengxiang Ding,Han Zhao,Yikai Qin,Xinhu Zheng,Donglin Wang,Yan Wang,Haoang Li*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: 提出一种新方法解决预训练VLA模型在标准监督微调中性能提升有限且适应成本高的问题，通过解耦辅助任务训练的两个目标，在参数空间中分离通用能力增强和任务特定动作分布拟合，只需在小规模任务集上训练即可获得能力向量，与预训练参数合并形成能力增强元模型。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 预训练的视觉语言动作模型在标准监督微调中往往难以有效提升性能并降低适应成本。虽然一些带有辅助训练目标的先进微调方法可以改善性能并减少收敛步数，但它们通常因辅助任务的额外损失而产生显著计算开销。

Method: 在参数空间中解耦辅助任务训练的两个目标：增强通用能力和拟合任务特定动作分布。只需在小规模任务集上使用两种不同训练策略使模型收敛，将得到的模型参数差异解释为辅助任务提供的能力向量。将这些向量与预训练参数合并形成能力增强元模型，并在标准SFT中加入轻量级正交正则化损失。

Result: 实验结果表明，该方法在各种机器人任务中非常有效，合并后的模型在减少计算开销的情况下，性能可与辅助微调的基线相媲美。

Conclusion: 该方法同时实现了辅助训练增强能力和标准SFT简单性的双重优势，通过解耦训练目标、提取能力向量并与预训练参数合并，显著提高了VLA模型的微调效率和性能。

Abstract: This paper proposes a novel approach to address the challenge that pretrained VLA models often fail to effectively improve performance and reduce adaptation costs during standard supervised finetuning (SFT). Some advanced finetuning methods with auxiliary training objectives can improve performance and reduce the number of convergence steps. However, they typically incur significant computational overhead due to the additional losses from auxiliary tasks. To simultaneously achieve the enhanced capabilities of auxiliary training with the simplicity of standard SFT, we decouple the two objectives of auxiliary task training within the parameter space, namely, enhancing general capabilities and fitting task-specific action distributions. To deliver this goal, we only need to train the model to converge on a small-scale task set using two distinct training strategies. The difference between the resulting model parameters can then be interpreted as capability vectors provided by auxiliary tasks. These vectors are then merged with pretrained parameters to form a capability-enhanced meta model. Moreover, when standard SFT is augmented with a lightweight orthogonal regularization loss, the merged model attains performance comparable to auxiliary finetuned baselines with reduced computational overhead. Experimental results demonstrate that this approach is highly effective across diverse robot tasks. Project page: https://chris1220313648.github.io/Fast-dVLA/

### [4] [SoftMimicGen: A Data Generation System for Scalable Robot Learning in Deformable Object Manipulation](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.25725)
*Masoud Moghani,Mahdi Azizian,Animesh Garg,Yuke Zhu,Sean Huver,Ajay Mandlekar*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: SoftMimicGen是一个用于可变形物体操作的自动化数据生成管道，通过高保真仿真环境生成合成数据集，减少对真实世界数据的需求，并支持多种机器人形态和操作任务。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 大规模机器人数据集难以收集和扩展，需要大量人力、时间和成本。虽然仿真和合成数据生成已成为有效替代方案，但现有方法主要局限于刚体任务，而可变形物体操作在真实世界操作中占据很大比例，这是合成仿真数据范式需要解决的关键空白。

Method: 提出SoftMimicGen自动化数据生成管道，包含一套高保真仿真环境，涵盖多种可变形物体（毛绒玩具、绳子、纸巾、毛巾）和操作行为（高精度穿线、动态挥舞、折叠、抓放），支持四种机器人形态：单臂机械臂、双臂机械臂、人形机器人和手术机器人。

Result: 应用SoftMimicGen在整个任务套件中生成数据集，从数据中训练高性能策略，并对数据生成系统进行系统分析。项目网站提供了详细信息。

Conclusion: SoftMimicGen填补了可变形物体操作领域合成仿真数据的空白，为机器人操作技能学习提供了可扩展的数据生成解决方案，有望推动合成仿真数据范式在更广泛机器人操作任务中的应用。

Abstract: Large-scale robot datasets have facilitated the learning of a wide range of robot manipulation skills, but these datasets remain difficult to collect and scale further, owing to the intractable amount of human time, effort, and cost required. Simulation and synthetic data generation have proven to be an effective alternative to fuel this need for data, especially with the advent of recent work showing that such synthetic datasets can dramatically reduce real-world data requirements and facilitate generalization to novel scenarios unseen in real-world demonstrations. However, this paradigm has been limited to rigid-body tasks, which are easy to simulate. Deformable object manipulation encompasses a large portion of real-world manipulation and remains a crucial gap to address towards increasing adoption of the synthetic simulation data paradigm. In this paper, we introduce SoftMimicGen, an automated data generation pipeline for deformable object manipulation tasks. We introduce a suite of high-fidelity simulation environments that encompasses a wide range of deformable objects (stuffed animal, rope, tissue, towel) and manipulation behaviors (high-precision threading, dynamic whipping, folding, pick-and-place), across four robot embodiments: a single-arm manipulator, bimanual arms, a humanoid, and a surgical robot. We apply SoftMimicGen to generate datasets across the task suite, train high-performing policies from the data, and systematically analyze the data generation system. Project website: \href{https://softmimicgen.github.io}{softmimicgen.github.io}.

</details>
