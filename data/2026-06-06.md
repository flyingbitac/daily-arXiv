### [1] [OLIVE: Online Low-Rank Incremental Learning for Efficient Adaptive Exoskeletons](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05234)
*Dong Liu,Yanxuan Yu,Ben Lengerich,Tony Geng,Ying Nian Wu*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: OLIVE是一个参数高效的在线自适应框架，通过低秩残差分解和奖励引导的策略梯度，使外骨骼控制系统在部署过程中持续个性化适应，在多种地形上实现步态平滑度、努力减少和运动稳定性的显著提升。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有可穿戴外骨骼控制器依赖静态步态策略，无法适应动态真实环境或个体用户特征。

Method: 将控制策略的自适应部分分解为低秩残差形式 ΔW = AB^T，通过基于传感器反馈（EMG、IMU、振动）的奖励引导策略梯度更新参数，并引入门控机制和动态秩调度器以适应不同地形复杂度。

Result: 在步态平滑度、努力减少和运动稳定性上分别比最强基线提高13、22和15个百分点，约1800步内收敛，端到端延迟7.4ms。

Conclusion: OLIVE框架实现了外骨骼控制的高效在线个性化适应，兼顾了稳定性和适应性，在多样化地形上表现鲁棒。

Abstract: Wearable exoskeleton systems hold promise for restoring mobility in individuals with physical impairments, yet most existing controllers rely on static gait policies that lack the ability to adapt to dynamic real-world environments or individual user characteristics. We present \olive (\underline{O}nline \underline{L}ow-rank \underline{I}ncremental Learning for Efficient Adapti\underline{ve} Exoskeletons), a parameter-efficient online adaptation framework that continuously personalizes exoskeleton control during deployment. \olive decomposes the adaptive component of the control policy into a low-rank residual form~$\dW = \At\Bt^\top$ with rank~$r!\ll!\min(d,k)$, reducing online update cost from $\mathcal{O}(dk)$ to $\mathcal{O}(r(d{+}k))$ while preserving the stability of a pretrained base controller~$\Wz$. Parameters are updated via a reward-shaped policy gradient driven purely by on-body sensor feedback (EMG, IMU, vibration), eliminating dependence on offline reference trajectories. A gating mechanism modulates the strength of personalization based on contextual state, and a dynamic rank scheduler adapts the update dimensionality to terrain complexity -- allocating minimal capacity on simple flat terrain and expanding to higher-rank updates on demanding uneven surfaces -- enabling robust performance across diverse activities: flat walking, stair navigation, slopes, and uneven terrain. Experiments on the wearable platform demonstrate that \olive achieves +13, +22, and +15 percentage-point improvements in gait smoothness, effort reduction, and motion stability over the strongest baseline, converging within $\sim$1{,}800 walking steps at 7.4,ms end-to-end latency. Our code implementation is available at https://github.com/FastLM/OLIVE.

### [2] [Inverse Manipulation through Symbolic Planning and Residual Operator Learning](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05248)
*Yigit Yildirim,Giuseppe Rauso,Riccardo Caccavale,Alberto Finzi*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: 提出了一种混合框架，通过从演示中自动提取STRIPS-like算子，结合符号规划和残差强化学习，实现机器人操作任务的可逆执行。在PushCube任务中，符号逆规划提供粗略恢复，残差SAC策略则细化物体位姿以满足剩余谓词条件。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 机器人操作任务中，单纯的符号逆规划或回放运动轨迹无法有效恢复连续交互动力学下的正向执行效果，需要一种结合符号推理与连续控制的方法来解决逆操作问题。

Method: 从演示中通过软几何谓词自动提取STRIPS-like算子，为每个算子构建逆恢复目标（保持前提条件、恢复删除效果、否定添加效果）；任务规划器尝试用动作原语满足该目标；未解决的符号谓词则通过强化学习（SAC策略）进行残差学习。

Result: 在ManiSkill3 PushCube任务上验证，正向推技能对应的符号逆规划执行粗略的抓放恢复，残差SAC策略进一步细化立方体姿态，满足剩余的逆谓词条件，将近似符号逆规划转化为物理可行的逆技能。

Conclusion: 谓词驱动的残差控制能够将近似的符号逆规划转化为物理上可执行的逆技能，混合框架有效结合了符号推理的离散性与强化学习的连续性。

Abstract: Inverting a robotic task requires more than reversing symbolic state transitions or rewinding motor trajectories. In robot manipulation tasks, symbolic inverse plans often fail to fully restore the effects of forward executions under continuous interaction dynamics. We present a hybrid framework for inverse manipulation that derives inverse-skill objectives from STRIPS-like operators automatically extracted from demonstrations through soft geometric predicates. For each extracted operator, we construct an inverse restoration objective that preserves preconditions, restores delete effects, and negates add effects. A task planner first attempts to satisfy this objective using available action primitives. Unresolved symbolic predicates then induce a residual operator learning problem solved through Reinforcement Learning (RL). We evaluate the framework on the ManiSkill3 PushCube task. For a forward pushing skill, the symbolic inverse performs a coarse pick-and-place restoration, while a residual Soft Actor-Critic policy refines the cube pose to satisfy the remaining inverse predicates. Our results show that predicate-derived residual control can turn an approximate symbolic inverse into a physically grounded inverse skill.

### [3] [Learning from Demonstrations over Riemannian Manifolds using Neural ODEs: An Extended Abstract](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05422)
*Diana Cuervo Espinosa,Mahathi Anand,Angela P. Schoellig*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: 本文提出利用神经常微分方程在黎曼流形上高效估计测地线，实现机器人位置与姿态轨迹的学习与生成。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有的示教学习方法通常在欧氏空间进行，但机器人状态（如方向）天然存在于弯曲空间（流形），需要一种能同时编码位置和方向数据的流形学习方法。

Method: 通过神经常微分方程（Neural ODE）数值估计黎曼流形上的测地线路径，避免传统方法的高计算开销，并将测地线解码回原始任务空间供机器人执行。

Result: 在仿真实验中与其它测地线计算方法进行了对比，验证了方法的有效性，但具体定量结果未在摘要中详述。

Conclusion: 基于神经常微分方程的黎曼流形示教学习能高效生成自然、复杂的机器人运动轨迹，未来需进一步解决相关挑战。

Abstract: Learning from demonstratins (LfD) is usually performed over Euclidean spaces, while the robot state, e.g. orientation, naturally evolves over curved spaces. Therefore, to ensure natural, complex motion generation, we investigate learning from demonstrations over Riemannian manifolds that are capable of encoding both position and orientation data. Here, geodesic paths provide for natural motion between two arbitrary points within the manifold. We propose to numerically estimate geodesics via neural ordinary differential equations, mitigating large computational overhead of existing approaches. Finally, these geodesics can be decoded back into the original task space before deploying on the robot. In this extended abstract, we discuss the architecture of our framework, provide some initial insights from our simulation experiments, including comparison to other geodesic computation mechanisms, and discuss the challenges and prospects for future work.

### [4] [Learning Contact Representation for Leg Odometry](https://arxiv.org/abs/2606.05501)
*Emre Girgin,Cagri Kilic*

Main category: cs.RO

TL;DR: 本文提出一种自监督表示学习框架，仅利用关节编码器实现足式机器人触地检测，无需力传感器，在性能上超越监督方法和基线概率方法。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 现有足式机器人里程计依赖触地检测，但力传感器并非普遍可用，且对滑移等干扰不敏感，因此需要一种不依赖力传感器的触地检测方法。

Method: 提出自监督表示学习框架，仅使用标准关节编码器传感器，学习表示对支撑相和摆动相进行概率建模，实现触地检测。

Result: 实验结果表明所提自监督触地检测器有效，性能优于需要力传感器标注的监督方法以及基线概率方法。

Conclusion: 自监督学习框架可替代力传感器实现高精度触地检测，代码已开源，为足式机器人提供更通用的触地检测方案。

Abstract: The estimation of odometry in legged robots depends on the assumption that the velocity of the foot with respect to the world remains zero during the stance phase. Feedback for the main body velocity is derived from the kinematic serial chain of the feet making accurate leg phase detection is a critical subproblem. A considerable number of studies employ ground reaction force sensors mounted at the tip of the foot to classify, yet these sensors may not be universally available for all legged robots. Additionally, these sensors are often unresponsive to unaccounted disturbances, such as slippage, while the foot remains in contact with the ground. In this study, we propose a self-supervised representation learning framework for contact detection that utilizes the standard sensor set of joint encoders without reliance on force sensor augmentations. We employ learned representations to model the stance and swing phases probabilistically. The experimental results obtained confirm the efficacy of the proposed self-supervised contact detector. Our framework exhibited superior performance in comparison to supervised methods which necessitate sensor set augmentation and labeling, as well as baseline probabilistic approaches. Additionally, we make our code available to the public.

</details>
